You are not eligible for the Registered Traveller Service

The Registered Traveller Service is not open to people who have ever:

  • been refused entry to the UK
  • had conditions imposed on their entry to the UK
  • had action taken against them for breaking customs laws
  • had a criminal conviction, including spent convictions or police caution

You can still enter the UK in the normal way.
